Carnival of Happiness

The student group organized a Carnival of Happiness to promote joy and mental well-being among their peers. They started by brainstorming fun activities and themes that could uplift spirits, such as games, workshops, and creative arts.

Each member took on specific responsibilities, from planning the event schedule to managing logistics and decorations. They reached out to local businesses and organizations for sponsorships and support, ensuring a variety of engaging activities.

The group designed colorful posters and used social media to promote the carnival, inviting students to join the festivities. On the day of the event, they set up booths for games, relaxation zones, and workshops focused on mindfulness and self-care.

Throughout the carnival, they encouraged participants to share what makes them happy and to engage in activities that fostered a sense of community. The event was a success, with many students expressing their appreciation for the positive atmosphere.

Finally, the group reflected on the experience, noting how organizing the Carnival of Happiness strengthened their teamwork skills and highlighted the importance of mental well-being.
